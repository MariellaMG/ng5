VENDOR APPLICATION

PLEASE NOTE

Your application does not guarantee a spot in the event. All applications go through a selection process. If you are selected, you will be notified via Instagram. When selected a link for payment will be sent to you. You will have 24 hours to pay for your spot. If not paid within 24 hours your spot will be open for the next vendor on the list.

This process takes place due to the high demand we have in participation.

In order to be considered as a vendor for Molcajete Dominguero, your items must be at least 80% Latino inspired. Food Vendors should have Latinx inspired plates Price is as follows:

MOLCAJETE DOMINGUERO EVENTS

  • $100 Per 6' Table Space, Bring your own WHITE 7' or 9' Patio Umbrella with Stand (No Canopies Allowed)
  • $300 Food Vendors, 10' x 14' space only. You must bring your own 10' x 10' Canopy - 4' Extra Space for cooking

COMIDA FEST (Every Sunday) - FOOD VENDORS

  • $300 Per 10' x 14' space if purchased on a per-week basis
  • $200 per 10' x 14' space if the whole month is purchased in advance
