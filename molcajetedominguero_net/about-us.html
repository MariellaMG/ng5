ABOUT US

Molcajete Dominguero was created for the Vendors by the Vendors. Together we share the passion to create an experience focused on bringing the local community and artist together.

When Vendors and Artist support each other amazing things can happen!

We are a one-of-a-kind Latinx shopping extravaganza that takes place every month. We have over 70 curated local artist and vendors. You will find clothing, jewelry, bath & body, paper goods, and much more!

Enjoy delicious Latinx food! Including Tacos, Esquites, Sweet Delights, Peruvian, Freshly brewed Iced coffee and much more.....

Enjoy an afternoon full of fun with friends and family.

Entrance is Free !
