CORE VALUES
Clear, Honest Communication We are committed to clarity, honesty and timeliness in all communication with customers, suppliers and fellow team members. We provide our team members with frequent, accurate, and up-to-date information, so that they have the knowledge to communicate effectively. Diversity We actively welcome and value people with different perspectives, backgrounds, cultures and experiences and we take the time to listen to and understand the viewpoints, issues, and concerns of all. We do this while working together as one Haliburton. Empowerment and Accountability We encourage our team members to take initiative and give their best while taking responsibility for actions that affect others. We strive to do the right thing, not just to do things right. Focus on Safety We strive to ensure the safety of our team members by giving them the resources and knowledge to protect themselves and others.
Profitable and Sustainable Growth We pursue training and professional development opportunities to ensure that we are knowledgeable, up-to-date, and continue to grow, both as a company and as individuals. We believe that profitability ensures the autonomy necessary to continually improve our ability to serve our customers and be the leader in our industry. Teamwork and Collaboration We are committed to working together in a positive way to get the job done, even if it’s “not our job”. We work to find solutions, not blame and always respect and acknowledge the contributions of our fellow team. Culinary and Technological Expertise We are passionate about culinary innovation and technology leadership. We demonstrate leadership in innovation through world class culinary, science and engineering. Eco-Conscious and Eco-Friendly We respect our environment and strive to save energy, conserve resources and maximize recycling wherever and whenever we can. Exceptional People, Exceptional Solutions We recognize that exceptional people are the source of all great innovation and encourage the free flow of thoughts, best practices and ideas throughout the company. We utilize the minds and capabilities of our team members to provide our customers with innovative solutions.
Leadership in Innovation and Quality We are committed to continuous quality improvement and innovative product design in all that we do, so that we will rank among the world’s leading food manufacturers in total customer satisfaction by understanding what our customers want, by helping them use our products to their maximum benefit and by executing flawlessly. Respect for Customers, Vendors and Team Members We conduct ourselves in a manner that reflects well upon the reputation of the company by giving due respect to ourselves and others. We strive to make each experience enjoyable, personal and hassle-free, while respecting each others time. Trust and Respect We strive to be a trusted partner, not just a vendor, customer or employer. We honor commitments always and treat all people equally with respect and integrity.
