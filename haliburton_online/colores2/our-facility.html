OUR FACILITY
With convenient access to California’s interstates and just 50 miles from the port of Long Beach, it’s an ideal location for distribution and manufacturing. We are also less than two miles from Ontario International Airport making travel to our facility a breeze. Our 300,000 sq. ft. manufacturing and distribution center is also home to the company’s main offices and innovation center. Constructed in 2011, the facility includes:
- Two fully equipped flexible commercial test kitchens

- Large 10 station research and development lab

- Certified on site microbiology lab featuring Dupont’s BAX Rapid Microbial testing system
- Fully equipped physical chemistry lab with the latest testing equipment

- Extensive USDA Certified Food Processing Facilities

- National distribution operations with more than 2.5 million cubic feet of cold storage capacity
