CAREERS

We make it our goal to provide top quality products and service to all of our customers. In order to achieve this, we need top- quality employees who take pride in their own performance and enjoy working for an innovative industry leader.

CAREERS
For every open position, we seek candidates
• Who are at “the top of their game”
• Who are looking for a challenging career, not just a job
• Who possess excellent interpersonal and communication skills
• Who embrace change and innovation
• Who can work independently while still collaborating as a team
Our work environment is casual, fast paced, fun and very customer focused. We offer full time employees an impressive benefits package including flexible health insurance options, medical savings plan, 401(k) savings plan with generous employer matching contributions, tuition reimbursement and life insurance. Best of all, with over 25 years in the food industry, as we continue to enjoy steady growth so do our team members.
If Haliburton sounds like a fit for you, email your resume to careers@haliburton.net today.
